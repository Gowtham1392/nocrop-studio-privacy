Privacy Policy – NoCrop Studio

Effective date: 2025/09/29
Developer: SK Apps and Games

1) Overview

NoCrop Studio (“the App”) is a simple photo framing tool that lets you fit any image into standard post sizes (1:1, 4:5, 9:16) and then save or share the result. We designed the App to work entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit personal data to our servers because we do not operate any servers for this App.

2) What data we collect

We do not collect any personal data. The App has no account system, no analytics, and no third-party advertising SDKs.

Data Safety summary (for Google Play):
  • Data collected: None
  • Data shared: None
  • Data processed on device: Photos you select for editing/export remain on your device

3) Permissions & how we use them

The App requests minimal permissions solely to provide core functionality:

  • Photos/Media (Android: READ_MEDIA_IMAGES) – to let you pick a photo from your library for editing.
  • Save to library – to export and save the final image into your device’s gallery/Photos app.
  • Share – to open your device’s system share sheet and let you send the exported image to other apps of your choice.
  • Temporary files – the App may create temporary files during export; these remain on your device and can be removed by the system or by uninstalling the App.

Note: If you choose to share an exported image with another app (e.g., a social app), that app’s own privacy policy will apply to the shared content.

4) On-device processing

All image editing, framing, and export happen locally on your device. We do not upload your photos to any server. The App can be used fully offline.

5) Third-party services

The App does not include analytics, ads, or external data collection SDKs. We rely on your operating system’s built-in photo picker, save, and share capabilities.

6) Data retention & deletion

  • Retention: We do not retain data on our servers. Exported images are saved to your device until you delete them.
  • Delete your data: You control all images in your device’s gallery. You may delete exported images at any time. Uninstalling the App also removes its local cache and temporary files.

7) Children’s privacy

The App is intended for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8) International users

Because we do not collect or transmit personal data, there are no cross-border data transfers associated with the App.

9) Security

All processing occurs on your device. Please keep your operating system up to date and protect your device with a passcode/biometrics to safeguard your photos and exports.

10) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect product or legal changes. Updates will be posted on this page with a new “Effective date.” If we introduce new features that affect privacy, we will update this document before or at the time of release.
